Fizz Buzz Challenge

Explanation

This program accepts an integer as Input and produces an output for every integer fom zero to the input number. It also replaces every number that is divisible by Divisor 1 with Phrase 1 and every number that is divisible by Divisor 2 with Phrase 2. Numbers that are divisible by both Divisor 1 and Divisor 2 will be replaced by a concatenation of both Phrase 1 and Phrase 2. Input, Divisor 1, and Divisor 2 are integers with a minimum value of 1 and a maximum value of 100.
